## 🎨 **Design System Used**

### **Colors**
- **Black**: #000000 (background)
- **Blue**: #3B82F6 (primary)
- **Green**: #10B981 (success)
- **Red**: #EF4444 (danger)
- **Gray**: #6B7280 (secondary)

### **Typography**
- **Font**: Inter / SF Pro Display
- **Hero**: 96px (executive metrics)
- **H1**: 72px (main titles)
- **H2**: 48px (sections)
- **Body**: 16px (content)

### **Effects**
- **Glassmorphism**: blur(40px) + rgba borders
- **Shadows**: Subtle, Apple-style
- **Transitions**: cubic-bezier(0.4, 0, 0.2, 1)
- **Animations**: Smooth fade-ins

### **Layout**
- **Max Width**: 1400px (executive), 1280px (main)
- **Padding**: Generous (8-12)
- **Gap**: Consistent (4-8)
- **Rounded**: 24-32px
